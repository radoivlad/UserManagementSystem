Using a job id, additional information can be retrieved (adding specific endpoint):
  • /*id* - receive data of a specific job, identified by id;
Activate interactive console (or use Postman) to perform additional operations in 'job' database, such as:
POST:
  • add a new job, insert information: id, name, domain and base salary;
PUT:
  • /*id*/*baseSalary* - insert a specific job id, and a new base salary, to update it;
DELETE:
  • /*id* - delete a specific job from the database, inputting id;
